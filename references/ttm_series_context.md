## Beyond Question Framework

**Definition**: The question that defines the episode BEFORE the guest is chosen.

The guest is the best person alive to answer this question — not the other way around.

**Test**: "Can only this guest answer this question this way?" If no → sharpen the question or find a better fit.

**How to use in validation**:
1. Identify which theme the candidate belongs to
2. Check their fit against the theme's Beyond Question
3. Validate their angle is distinct from confirmed guests in the same theme

## Dual Layer

Every episode must work on two levels:
- **Surface**: Practical AI literacy — what to do, how to adapt
- **Deep**: Identity / worldview shift — how to *think* differently

## Theme Notes (for Section 6 analysis)

**Theme 1 — After Coding**: CS education angle. Coding as a skill vs. coding as a way of thinking.
**Theme 2 — Redesigning Learning**: Broader education/knowledge access. Drew = education angle, Po-Shen = math/democratization. Distinct from Theme 1.
**Theme 3 — When AI Dreams**: Cognition, neuroscience, creativity, social intelligence. The last frontier of human uniqueness.
**Theme 4 — The Future of Work**: Productivity, workplace transformation, labor markets. Does AI actually reduce work?
**Theme 5 — A New Map of Power**: Geopolitics, economics, macro AI dynamics. External/macro — countries, companies, economic structures.
**Theme 6 — The Architects**: AI values, alignment, design philosophy. Internal/micro — people shaping AI's character. Distinct from Theme 5.
