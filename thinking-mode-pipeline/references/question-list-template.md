## Question Design Rules

### Hierarchy Markers

| Mark | Meaning | Guideline |
|:----:|---------|-----------|
| 🔴 | Must ask — never skip | Missing this = lesson core is lost |
| ⚪ | Can skip — if time is short | Good but not essential |
| 🔵 | Series signature — recurring | Same question every episode, different answers |
| → | Follow-up — when answer is shallow | Indented under main question |

### Question Count Guide

| Interview Length | 🔴 per Lesson | ⚪ per Lesson | Total |
|:---------------:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 30 min | 2-3 | 0-1 | 8-10 |
| 45 min | 3-4 | 1 | 12-15 |
| 60 min+ | 3-4 | 1-2 | 14-18 |

### Quality Checklist

```
□ Every question is bilingual (English + Korean)
□ Every Lesson has ≥2 🔴 questions
□ Every 🔴 has ≥1 → follow-up
□ No generic questions ("What do you think about AI?")
□ Every question is guest-specific (only THIS person can answer)
□ Emotion-eliciting questions included (feelings, not just facts)
□ At least 1 counter-question (challenge their position)
□ Staleness filter passed (not repeating what they've said 3+ times)
□ Screen share moments identified with 🖥️
□ Timeline adds up to target interview length
□ Sources section includes all referenced materials with links
```
