# Outreach Email Template — The Thinking Mode

## Structure

The email has 3 paragraphs. Keep it under 200 words total. Professional but warm.

---

## Template

**Subject:** Invitation to EO Global's "The Thinking Mode" — {GUEST_NAME}

Hi {FIRST_NAME},

**[P1: Channel Intro + Credibility]**
My name is {YOUR_NAME}, and I'm a {YOUR_TITLE} at {CHANNEL_NAME}, a YouTube channel focused on {CHANNEL_FOCUS}. We have over {SUBSCRIBER_COUNT} subscribers, and our recent series "{SERIES_NAME}" explores {SERIES_DESCRIPTION}. Our first episode with {BREAKOUT_GUEST} reached over {BREAKOUT_VIEWS} views, and we've featured guests from {NOTABLE_AFFILIATIONS}.

**[P2: Personalized Reason — CUSTOMIZE THIS]**
{PERSONALIZED_PARAGRAPH}

> - Why THIS person fits the series
> - Specific connection to their current work/project/book
> - What unique perspective they bring that no one else can

## Customization Guide

### P2 Must Include:
1. **Specific reference** to guest's recent work (book, paper, talk, project)
2. **Timeliness hook** — why now (new publication, current events, industry moment)
3. **Series fit** — how their story connects to "Unlearn to Rewire" / "Practical Optimism"
4. **Unique angle** — what EO can draw out that other channels haven't

### Tone Rules:
- Professional but genuine (not corporate, not fan-mail)
- Show you've done your homework (cite specific work, not generic praise)
- Respect their time (keep it short)
- No jargon, no buzzwords
- Clearly state what's in it for them (audience reach, unique angle)

### Subject Line Variations:
- "Invitation to EO Global's 'The Thinking Mode' — {NAME}"
- "EO Global Interview Invitation — The Thinking Mode Series"
- "{NAME} × EO Global — 'The Thinking Mode' Series"

### Follow-up (if no response after 7 days):
- Shorter (3-4 sentences)
- Add one new hook (e.g., recent news that connects to their work)
- Reference the original email
